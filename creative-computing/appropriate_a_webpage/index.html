EROS
Magazine
(appropriated)

Eros Magazine was first published in the 1960s by Ralph Ginzburg. The magazine, which focused on eroticism, love, and the history of sexuality only lasted 5 issues, (only 4 of which were published.) Ginzburg was indicted on charges of "obscene advertising."

All four printed issues of the magazine were fully digitized and placed online this year by Mindy Seu.

You can find them here
